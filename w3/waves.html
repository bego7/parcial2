Welcome to waves-lab

High Performance Computing

In waves-lab use the incredible power of GPU's supercomputers to help solve some of the most complex technical, scientific, and commercial problems that organizations face.

We expect that parallel computing will lead to breakthroughs in science, engineering, modeling and simulation, healthcare, deep learning, robotics, bigdata, IoT and blockchain applications.

Applications

We are focusing on putting the GPU's computers to work solving industry-scale classification, machine learning, and optimization problems with our customers and collaborators. These complex problems exist across many domains such as:

  • Deep learning
  • Atomic scale materials modelling
  • Sampling / Monte Carlo
  • Pattern recognition and anomaly detection
  • Blockchain and IA applications
  • Image analysis
  • Bioinformatics / cancer research
